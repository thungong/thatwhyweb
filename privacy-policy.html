Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

ThatWhy ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data — in compliance with applicable privacy laws including the Thailand Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (PDPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

1. About This Website

This website (thatwhy.me) is a static informational site. We do not operate any user accounts, login systems, or first-party analytics on this website. The primary ways information reaches us are through direct email contact and infrastructure-level logs maintained by our hosting provider.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you contact us via email, you may provide:

  • Your name and email address
  • Company name and professional details
  • The content of your message or inquiry

We only receive what you choose to share with us.

2.2 Infrastructure & Access Logs

This website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. When you visit, Cloudflare may automatically record standard server log data including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages requested and timestamps
  • HTTP referrer headers

These logs are retained and managed by Cloudflare under their own Privacy Policy. We do not have direct access to raw server logs.

2.3 Third-Party Resources

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). When your browser fetches these fonts, Google's servers receive your IP address and browser information. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details. We do not use advertising networks, social media pixels, or behavioral tracking tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide through email contact is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or service request
  • Communicate about a potential or ongoing engagement
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your contact information for unsolicited marketing.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For visitors in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing personal data is:

  • Legitimate interests — responding to your direct inquiry
  • Contractual necessity — when processing relates to a service engagement
  • Legal obligation — where required by applicable law

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Infrastructure providers: Cloudflare (hosting), as described above
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, with appropriate notice

6. Cookies

This website does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party services (Google Fonts, Cloudflare) may set cookies or use similar technologies as part of their infrastructure. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Data Retention

Email correspondence is retained for as long as necessary for the business purpose it was collected for, or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of information we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent

Under Thailand's PDPA, you additionally have the right to be informed when your personal data is processed and to lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Committee (PDPC).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@thatwhy.me. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 20 years of age (in accordance with Thailand's PDPA) or 18 in other jurisdictions. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently received data from a minor, please contact us to have it removed.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed or stored in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including relying on service providers with adequate data protection standards.

11. Security

We rely on Cloudflare's enterprise-grade infrastructure for security. Email communications are standard internet email. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites (GitHub, portfolio projects). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies independently.

13. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our practices evolve. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
